Who We Are

Satinova was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create timeless fashion that empowers individuals to express their unique style with confidence. Our journey began in a small atelier in Paris, where our founder's passion for elegant design and quality craftsmanship laid the foundation for what Satinova is today.
